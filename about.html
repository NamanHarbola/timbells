My story

Where it all started

It started with a single crochet hook and way too much free time. What began as a lockdown hobby quickly turned into a genuine passion — I couldn't stop making things, and people couldn't stop asking "wait, you made that yourself?!"

I work across crochet, pipe cleaner crafts, acrylic & watercolour painting, clay modelling, and embroidery. Each medium has its own personality, and I love switching between them.

Through community workshops around Delhi, I've discovered how much I love helping others find their own creative spark. Watching someone make their first crochet stitch or finish their first bouquet? That never gets old.
